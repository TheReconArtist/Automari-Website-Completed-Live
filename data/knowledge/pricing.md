# Automari Pricing

Automari's pricing is tailored to your specific business needs and the scope of automation required. Generally, our solutions range from **$1,500 to $10,000+ per month**.

Key factors influencing the price include:

*   **Complexity of Automation:** Simple, single-process automations are at the lower end, while multi-system integrations are higher.
*   **Data Volume:** The amount of data processed by our AI agents.
*   **Number of Integrations:** How many of your existing systems (CRM, ERP, etc.) need to connect with our AI.
*   **Customization Level:** The degree of bespoke development required for unique workflows.
*   **Ongoing Support & Optimization:** Different tiers of continuous monitoring and refinement.
